About Our Team

We are a passionate robotics team focused on designing and building high-performance rover systems. Our mission is to learn, compete, and push the limits of student engineering.

Who We Are

Our team brings together students from different engineering backgrounds to design mechanical systems, embedded control, autonomous features, communication systems, and field-ready robots.

Through competitions and real-world projects, we improve our technical skills, collaboration, and innovation mindset.
